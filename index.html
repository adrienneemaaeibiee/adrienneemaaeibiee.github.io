The QUT Bookshops remain open and we would love to see you.

Unfortunately due to impact of COVID-19, the GP Bookshop Café is closed for the remainder of 2020.
If you hold a prepaid QUT Bookshop & Café Campos coffee card with an unused balance, pop into the GP Bookshop for an equivalent credit.

We value your support during these difficult times. Our utmost priority is to keep our staff and customers safe and reduce any health risks when visiting our store.

Hand sanitiser dispensers have been installed at, or close to, the entrance of both stores. We have increased our cleaning and disinfecting of all high touch surfaces. To further reduce risk, please pay by card.

Stay safe, look after one another and catch up on your reading.
